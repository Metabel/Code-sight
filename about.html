About She Code Kenya

At She Code Kenya, we empower girls and teach them coding skills. Our aim to deconstuct the norm that STEM related courses are for the male gender and that girls can thrive too in the field of tech.

Mission

The overarching mission of various "tech on girls" initiatives is to empower and support women and girls in pursuing careers and education in STEM fields, ultimately aiming to close the gender gap in the technology sector and diversify the industry. This is achieved through a variety of approaches, including providing access to education, resources, and mentorship, as well as promoting awareness and combating online violence and harassment.

What We Offer

  • Hands on training on full stack development
  • AI ,Data Science
  • Mentorship

Our Journey

Founded in 2015, She Code Kenya has trained over 15000 girls who are thriving in various fields.

Vision

The vision for technology impacting girls involves empowering them through digital skills, promoting STEM education, and creating safe online spaces. This vision aims to ensure that girls can fully participate in the digital economy, develop future-ready skills, and contribute to innovation
